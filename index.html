Fun & engaging speech practice

Designed to build real vocabulary through play — not mindless scrolling.

🍎

Object Naming

Toddlers practice naming everyday things like "milk," "apple," and "shoe." The more words they recognize, the more they can ask for what they need.

🖼️

AI Flashcard Overlays NEW

Beautiful flashcards pop up right over whatever your child is doing, in 24 stunning art styles — without interrupting their videos or games!

🎨

Descriptive Words

Kids learn "red ball," "big ball," "soft ball" — the building blocks of describing the world around them.

🏃

Action Words

The AI narrates simple actions like "putting on shoes" or "washing hands," helping toddlers learn verbs for asking what they want.

🎙️

27 Friendly Voices

Different voices, accents, and tones help kids recognize words across many speakers — a skill that makes vocabulary stick.

🌟

Daily Affirmations

A warm moment at the end of each session. Phrases like "I am brave" and "I can try" leave kids feeling good about practice.

🔢

Counting & Alphabet

Gentle repetition builds counting from 1 to 100, plus the letters of the alphabet, at your child's pace.

🐰

Movement Breaks

A cheerful AR character invites kids to stretch, hop, and move between sessions — healthy daily routines through play.

📈

Progress Log

When your child says a word, the app celebrates and notes it in your weekly summary so you can see vocabulary building over time.
