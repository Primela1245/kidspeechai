Frequently asked

Common questions

Is this a replacement for a speech therapist?
No. Kid Speech AI is a daily-practice companion — not therapy and not a replacement for a licensed speech-language pathologist. If you're worried about your child's speech development, please connect with a professional. Kid Speech AI is designed to make the time between professional visits playful and productive.
What age is this for?
The app is designed for toddlers ages 3 to 5 who are building early vocabulary. Every child develops at their own pace; please consult your pediatrician if you have concerns about your child's speech development.
Is my child's data safe?
Yes. Kid Speech AI is designed for the Google Play Families program and follows COPPA and GDPR-K data practices. We don't sell data. We don't show ads. Audio is encrypted in transit and is not used to train AI models. You can delete your data anytime from inside the app or by emailing support@kidspeechai.com.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Your subscription is handled by Google Play. You can cancel anytime from the Play Store before your trial ends to avoid being charged, or anytime after to stop future renewals. You keep access until the end of your current billing period.
How does the AI work?
Kid Speech AI uses an AI character that speaks age-appropriate words and phrases from a curated, child-safe library. All audio is filtered before it reaches your child — no scary, adult, or off-topic content. The AI is clearly introduced to parents as a computer program, not a therapist or a real person.
What if my child has a diagnosed speech condition?
Please consult a licensed speech-language pathologist for clinical guidance. Kid Speech AI is built for playful, daily vocabulary practice — it's not a treatment and shouldn't replace professional care.
