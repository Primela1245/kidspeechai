Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

Quick summary: Kid Speech AI is built for kids. We collect the minimum data needed to make the app work, we never sell your data, we never show ads, and parents can delete everything anytime. We follow COPPA (United States) and GDPR-K (European Union) rules for children's privacy.

1. Who we are

Kid Speech AI is an educational mobile application for toddlers ages 3 to 5. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when a parent or guardian sets up an account and a child uses the app. References to 'we,' 'us,' and 'our' mean Kid Speech AI. Contact: support@kidspeechai.com.

2. Parental consent and accounts

The app is intended to be set up and managed by a parent or legal guardian. By creating an account, you confirm that you are at least 18 years old and that you are the parent or guardian of any child profile you create. We do not knowingly collect information directly from children without parental consent.

3. Information we collect

From parents (account holders)

  • Email address — for account creation, password recovery, and important service messages.
  • Subscription status — provided by Google Play. We do not see your full payment details.
  • Support correspondence — if you contact us by email.

From child profiles

  • First name and age — used inside the app to personalize the experience.
  • In-app activity — words attempted, lessons completed, time spent in the app. Used to power the parent progress dashboard.
  • Voice recordings — when the child speaks during practice, audio is processed for speech recognition. See Section 6 for how we handle audio.

Technical information

  • Device identifier and operating system — for analytics and crash reporting.
  • Crash logs — to help us fix bugs.

4. What we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not access photos, videos, or files on the device.
  • We do not access contacts, calendar, or messages.
  • We do not collect biometric data.
  • We do not collect health or medical information.
  • We do not allow children to message other users — there are no social features.

5. How we use information

  • To provide and improve the app experience.
  • To show parents progress reports.
  • To process subscriptions through Google Play Billing.
  • To respond to support requests.
  • To diagnose crashes and bugs.

We do not use children's data for advertising. We do not show third-party ads. We do not sell or rent data to anyone.

6. Audio (voice recordings)

The app uses speech recognition to celebrate when your child says words. To do this, short audio clips may be sent to a speech-recognition service for processing. We work with a vendor that does not use your child's audio to train their AI models. Audio is encrypted in transit. We do not store audio longer than is necessary to process a single practice lesson. Parents can disable voice features in Settings.

7. Service providers we share data with

We share the minimum necessary data with the following types of service providers, under contracts that limit their use of the data to providing services to us:

  • Cloud hosting — to store account data and progress logs.
  • Speech recognition — to process voice audio during lessons.
  • Crash reporting and analytics — configured in child-directed mode (no advertising identifiers, no personalized advertising).
  • Google Play Billing — for subscription processing.

We do not share data with advertising networks. We do not allow third parties to use your child's data for their own marketing.

8. Your rights — parents and guardians

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about your account and child profile.
  • Correction — fix anything that's wrong.
  • Deletion — delete the account and all associated data anytime. See our account deletion page.
  • Refuse further collection — stop using the app or revoke microphone permission in your device settings.
  • Portability — request your data in a portable format.

To exercise any of these rights, email support@kidspeechai.com. We respond within 30 days.

9. Data retention

We keep account and progress data for as long as the account is active. When you delete your account, we delete the data within 30 days, except where we are required to keep it (e.g., basic payment records for tax purposes, which are anonymized).

10. Children's privacy — COPPA and GDPR-K

Kid Speech AI is designed for the Google Play Designed for Families program. We comply with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the EU General Data Protection Regulation as it applies to children (GDPR-K). We do not knowingly collect personal information from a child without verifiable parental consent (account creation by a parent counts as that consent).

If you believe we have collected information from your child without your consent, please email support@kidspeechai.com and we will delete it.

11. Security

We use industry-standard encryption (TLS/HTTPS) for data in transit and at rest. We restrict employee access to personal data on a need-to-know basis. No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable steps to protect your data.

12. International transfers

We are based in the United States. If you use the app from outside the U.S., your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States, which may have different data-protection laws than your country.

13. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we will notify account holders by email and update the 'Last updated' date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

14. Contact us

Questions about privacy? Email support@kidspeechai.com. We'll respond within 3 business days.
