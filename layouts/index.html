Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a replacement for a speech therapist?

AI Kid Therapy is a gentle supplement to professional therapy, not a replacement. It provides fun daily practice and reinforcement between sessions — or serves as an affordable starting point for families on waitlists.

What age is this for?

The app is designed for toddlers ages 3–5 with speech delay, autism spectrum, or who are late talkers. The AI glasses are built for kids ages 3–8. Always consult your pediatrician for clinical guidance.

When will the AI glasses ship?

We begin production once we reach our $10,000 funding goal. We're building these from scratch — estimated delivery is 4–6 months after the goal is met. Beta testers get first shipment priority.

Is there a screen on the glasses?

No. The glasses are completely audio-only — no screen, no display, no additional screen time. A camera identifies objects and the AI speaks to your child through built-in speakers. Zero screen time added.

Is this safe screen time for my toddler?

Yes! We built AI Kid Therapy to be the exact opposite of mindless scrolling. The app uses the camera to look outward at your child's toys and real world, turning physical play into an interactive vocabulary lesson. It's screen time you can feel genuinely good about.

Can I get a refund on the glasses pre-order?

Yes — full refund available anytime before production begins. Once manufacturing starts, we offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee after delivery.
